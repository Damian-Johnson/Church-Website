Church of Christ Truths

  • Founded by Christ - Matt 16:18
  • Jesus is the Head - Eph 1:22-23, Col 1:18
  • Christ is the only true foundation - 1 Cor 3:11, Acts 4:12
  • Bible is the only guide - 1 Tim 3:16-17, 1 Peter 4:11, Gal 1:6-9
  • The church is the body of Jesus - Col 1:18
  • The church is not a building; the church consists of Christians - Eph 5:29-30
  • There is one body - Eph 4:4-6
  • Jesus is the head of one church - Mat 16:18
  • We can be saved through scriptural baptism - Gal 3:26-29, 1 Peter 3:21


Our Mission given by our Lord is to teach the word of God so others may hear words by which they can be saved. (Matthew 28:19-20) (Acts 11:14)
The Apostles, guided by the Holy Spirit show us the pattern for the church and how Christians should behave in striving to teach only the truth, which is the word of God, not Man's word. (Jn 14:26, 16:13) (Jn 4:24) (Jn 17:17)
In order to accomplish this, we recognize the need to search the Scriptures and to speak only that which is authorized by the word of God.
(Acts 17:11) (1Pe 4:11) (Col 3:17)
